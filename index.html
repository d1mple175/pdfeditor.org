About PDFeditor

Author: Rizq-Azamirol

Release Date: 2025

Purpose: A standalone, offline-capable PDF merging tool designed for users who value both functionality and unconventional aesthetics. This application allows you to merge multiple PDF files, reorder them via drag-and-drop, preview documents before merging, and track merge progress — all within your browser without any data being uploaded to external servers.

Technical Stack: PDF-Lib, SortableJS, FileSaver.js

Design Philosophy: Dream Brut · Weirdcore · Neubrutalism
